Mod Multiverse

A modding site with an infinite selection of mods, for an infinite selection of games, with infinite protection against theft.

About

Mod Multiverse aims to provide mods for every game, while protecting the mod developer's work from theft.
This is achieved by protecting the mod on three levels:
  1. Protecting the file
  2. Protecting the download
  3. Supreme protection

Files

File are protected using de facto mod locking systems. But these can be defeated. So we supplement them with our proprietary system.

SecuMOD
SecuMOD provides undefeatable levels of protection. Even the NSA keeps begging us to implement a backdoor. (We won't. Promise.)
The file is protected in the following ways:
File Encryption
Files are encrypted with 1,024 rounds of AES with a 4,096 bit key, followed by 512 rounds of ChaCha with a 1,024 bit key.
Anti-Tamper
Attempting to reverse engineer, debug, dump, rip, screen capture or do virtually anything other than play the game will result in the game crashing. The mod deleting itself. Ransomware being installed onto the system. Your porn collection and any/all nudes being forwarded to your family, friends, boss, co-workers, teacher, classmates.
Monitoring
Undisclosed monitoring will be performed by the Modding Security Agency (MSA) to ensure that files are not being misused.

Downloads

Downloads are protected by layering authentication and by verifying the identity of the downloader. The following are required for someone to download a mod:
  • An account
  • A linked Steam/Origin/Uplay/Epic account (depending on the game)
  • A linked Discord account
  • A linked Patreon account (with subscription to any membership level)
  • A linked PayPal account
  • A copy of your birth certificate
  • A photo/scan of a passport/drivers licence/government ID card
  • Your credit history
  • Your bank details
  • Any credit card details
